  5. MAT 201
  6. MAT 203

PDP

People Democratic Party,this type of student are usually awarded another certificate apart from the BSC and their school fees is always more than that of the Regular Student
The following are the requirements

  1. O level
  2. 250 in JAMB
  3. Birth Certificate
  4. Citizenship Certificate
